About Dian

Discipline, precision,
and a personal touch.

Dian grew up in Gansbaai on the Western Cape coast, where he developed an early appreciation for hard work and craftsmanship. He went on to serve in the South African Marines, an experience that instilled in him the values that define his work today: precision, reliability, and an unwavering commitment to getting the job done right.

After the military, Dian channelled that same discipline into the fitness industry, running his own gym and coaching clients — developing a deep ability to listen, understand what people need, and deliver results they can see.

That combination — military precision and personal attentiveness — is exactly what he brings to every construction and remodeling project. When you work with Dian, you work directly with the person responsible for the outcome.
